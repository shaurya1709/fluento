Gradients

vibra design strongly discourages use of gradients. they do look good at some places however, they are not the best for a uI. instead we recommend solid colors

Emoji

the vibra design api uses tweMoji as it is consistent with no compactibility issues and fits in well with the style of vibra design. there is no setup. just connect the scripts mentioned in the requirements
